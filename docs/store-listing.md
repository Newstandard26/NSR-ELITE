**Description** (≤4000):
```
NSR Elite is the field canvassing app for New Standard Restoration's sales team. Sign in with your company account to map your territory, track every door you knock, and turn conversations into booked appointments.

MAP & CANVASS
• See your assigned territory and leads on a live map
• Drop a pin on any house to log a knock in seconds
• Set a disposition (not home, callback, appointment, not interested, and more)
• See your own location while you work — used only while the app is open

LEADS & APPOINTMENTS
• Capture homeowner details, notes, and photos on each lead
• Book inspections, estimates, and follow-ups
• Pull property details to qualify a home before you knock

TEAM & PERFORMANCE
• Leaderboards and daily/weekly targets to keep crews motivated
• Manager tools for territories, assignments, and team usage insights

CRM SYNC
• Push qualified leads to AccuLynx and keep them in sync

NSR Elite is provided to authorized New Standard Restoration staff and requires a company login.
```
